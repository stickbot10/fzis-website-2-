Nurturing Young Minds in Islamic Education

Fatima Zahra Islamic School (FZIS) is dedicated to providing quality Islamic education to children every Saturday from 9:30am to 12:30pm.

  • Professional Quran recitation lessons with qualified teachers using books created by Sheikh Muhammad Dehnavi
  • Comprehensive Deniyat curriculum covering Islamic beliefs and practices through the Al-Kisa Curriculum
  • Congregational Salah after classes to reinforce practical worship and community spirit
